Event Overview

Afrobeats Indoor Cycling: Vienna Takeover 2025 is a first-of-its-kind indoor fitness concert merging rhythm, culture, and performance. Building on the success of the 2024 pilot at John Reed Donauzenturm, this edition expands production quality, reach, and participation through a full-day format with four cycling sessions.

Transforming the Sport Arena Vienna into a high-energy, concert-style fitness mecca, the event combines live DJ sets, professional lighting, synchronized stands, and special effects to create a truly immersive atmosphere.

With 400 riders across four sessions and an expected total attendance of 800-1000 guests throughout the day, Afrobeats Indoor Cycling: Vienna "Takeover 2025 stands as Austria's largest indoor group-fitness cycling experience — a one-of-a-kind celebration of music, movement, and culture.
