ABOUT US

Redefining how rhythm, performance, and culture come together.
Ginger & Co. creates immersive, music-powered group-fitness experiences that inspire energy, connection, and community. Driven by a vision to redefine boutique fitness in Vienna through cultural rhythm, premium wellness, and inclusive lifestyle experiences, Ginger & Co. is on a mission to build Austria's first Afro-modern boutique studio — a space designed to deliver transformational fitness powered by music, movement, and community. Standing at the intersection of wellness and culture, Ginger & Co. connects people from diverse backgrounds through high-energy programs and signature events such as Afrobeats Indoor Cycling, while shaping a new era of culturally inspired fitness in Europe.

AFROBEATS INDOOR CYCLING: VIENNA TAKEOVER 2025

Austria's Largest Indoor Group-Fitness Cycling Experience to Date

An immersive pop-up indoor cycling event – music, movement, and magic. Afrobeats Indoor Cycling: Vienna Takeover 2025 is a first-of-its-kind indoor fitness concert merging rhythm, culture, and performance.
